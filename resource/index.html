Vmap1.0

A genus-level genetic variation map of wheat (VMap 1.0) performed whole-genome sequencing of 414 accessions from 25 subspecies of wheat relatives in the genus Triticum and Aegilopss. This is the first time to construct a genus-level whole-genome genetic variation map in all crops. The genetic variation map of the genus Triticum (VMap 1.0) will be very useful to wheat genetic study and breeding.
